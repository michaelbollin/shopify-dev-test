### Explanation

Liquid handles follow specific formatting rules to ensure consistency and proper functionality.

Key points:
- Handles must contain only:
  - Lowercase letters
  - Numbers
  - Hyphens
- Cannot contain:
  - Spaces
  - Special characters
  - Uppercase letters
- Examples of valid handles:
  - `product-title`
  - `featured-collection-123`
  - `main-menu`
- Used in various contexts like:
  - Section IDs
  - Block IDs
  - Collection handles
  - Product handles
